About Us

Third Generation Semiconductor (DongGuan) Co., Ltd is a leading manufacturer of advanced semiconductor materials. With more than 10 years of export experience, we have long-term customers in Europe, America and Southeast Asia.

We focus on GaN, AlN, SiC and diamond wafer & epitaxial wafer materials, providing customized material solutions for power electronics, RF devices and related high-tech applications.

Our main business covers semiconductor materials and semiconductor equipment.

Product range includes:

  • Semiconductor substrates: silicon, sapphire, gallium nitride, silicon carbide, aluminum nitride, gallium oxide, and diamond crystal substrate (wafer)
  • Epitaxial materials (epi-wafer)
  • Chips, LEDs, components
  • High-temperature consumables: pyrometer, carbon, graphite, graphite soft felt, crucible, etc.
  • Equipment: Rapid Thermal Processing Furnace (RTP), grinding and polishing machine, etc.
